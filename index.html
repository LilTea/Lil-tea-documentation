Introduction

Language basics

Stack

LilTea is a stack-based language with a single stack. Every operations performs a action on the stack

Atoms

Atoms are built-in functions making solutions quite easier and faster. They are the construction of the language

lilTea symbol bigTea symbol Explanation
+ plus Pops the top 2 values and pushes their sum
- minus Pops the top 2 values and pushes their difference
* mult Pops the top 2 values and pushes their product
/ div Pops the top 2 values and pushes their quotient
% mod Pops the top 2 values and pushes their their remainder after division
i ascsort Sorts the top array in ascending order
¢ dscsort Sorts the top array in descending order
£ square Pops the top value and pushes it's square
¤ sqrt Pops the top value and pushes it's square root
¥ dup Pushes the top value
¦ tan Pops the top value and pushes it's tan
© sin Pops the top value and pushes it's sin
¬ cos Pops the top value and pushes it's cos
® empty Pops everything
µ lenght Pushes the length of the stack
